Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of bread
  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Instructions:

1. Cook bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
3. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of each slice of bread.
4. Arrange cooked bacon and sliced tomatoes over the cheese on 4 slices of bread. Top with the remaining bread slices, buttered side up.
5. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Place sandwiches in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides and the cheese is melted, about 3 minutes per side.
6. Serve hot and enjoy!
